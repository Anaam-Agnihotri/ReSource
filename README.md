What is ReSource?
ReSource is an AI-powered sustainable living assistant designed to help people make smarter use of resources that are often wasted in everyday life. Instead of treating waste as something to simply throw away, ReSource helps users identify ways materials can be reused, recycled, repurposed, or even regrown. Whether it is leftover food, biodegradable waste, or plastics, the goal is simple — turn waste into opportunity.

What problem does it solve?
Modern resource depletion is not caused only by overconsumption but also by lack of awareness. Large amounts of food, biodegradable materials, and plastics are discarded despite having potential value. ReSource aims to solve this by providing accessible knowledge and AI-driven recommendations on sustainable practices such as composting, reusing materials, waste segregation, recycling methods, and alternative uses for common household waste.

Who is it meant for?
ReSource is built for individuals, households, students, environmentally conscious communities, and anyone who wants to adopt more sustainable habits without needing expert-level knowledge. It is meant for people who want practical guidance to reduce waste, conserve resources, and contribute toward a more circular and sustainable lifestyle.
